Intro

With over 15 years working in engineering teams and 20 years in the financial services sector, I create environments in which engineers are set up to succeed. My expertise in cloud platforms, test automation frameworks and building teams of engineers allow me to lead initiatives that improve quality, increase speed, and reduce costs.

Through my work, I bridge the gap between technology and business, ensuring engineering solutions deliver quality outcomes, supporting the overall mission of an organisation.

My commitment to learning through failure driving continuous improvement is at the core of everything I do, and key to making a positive impact on my team's performance, wellbeing, and purpose.
